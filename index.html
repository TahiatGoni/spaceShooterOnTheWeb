Tahiat's Space Shooter

Welcome!

As the top pilot in your planet, You are tasked with helping the evacuation efforts.
Hold back the Zoobs as long as you can to ensure that more people can evacuate to safety.

Controls: move up, move down, shoot.

This is my first project using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The motivation for this
project comes from putting everything I have learned so far to the test.

Special thanks to Chris Courses for his tutorials that helped me
